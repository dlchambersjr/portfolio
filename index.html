About Me

When I was 13 years old, I was introduced to the possibilities of what a computer could do, and more importantly, how it could be used to create something that could entertain, educate, or make a difference in the lives of people. I also discovered how much I liked those possibilities, so I created software programs to teach spelling words to elementary age children, to help student drivers in Germany learn foreign road signs, and a computer dating program that raised money for my high school.

I have served in the United States Air Force, spent 25 years as a youth and senior Pastor, and was the president of an Event Planning Company. During those years I saw the value of integrating coding into everyday tasks and accomplishing big picture goals.

I am a software developer specializing in web and JavaScript development. During the last 25 years of professional leadership experience building teams in both nonprofit and for-profit industries, I have developed a deep passion to see my company, its clients, and partners succeed by achieving their full potential through software development
